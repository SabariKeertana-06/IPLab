Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a symbol of enduring love and architectural splendor, graces the banks of the Yamuna River
in Agra, India. Commissioned by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz
Mahal, this marble masterpiece stands as a testament to the profound beauty of eternal devotion.

UNESCO World Heritage Site, attracting millions of visitors who are captivated by its timeless elegance.
